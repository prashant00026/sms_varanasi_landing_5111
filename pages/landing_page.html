Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about programs, admissions, and career outcomes

Average students see 170% salary increase within 2 years of graduation. With an average starting package of ₹12.5 LPA and program fee of ₹8.5 lakhs, most students recover their investment within 12-18 months. Our 95% placement rate and strong industry connections ensure excellent career outcomes.

Yes, SMS Varanasi is fully approved by AICTE and recognized by UGC. We hold NAAC A+ accreditation and are ranked #15 among B-Schools in India. All our programs are government-approved and degrees are valid for higher education and employment worldwide.

We offer a placement assurance program where students who complete the program with 75%+ attendance and pass all assessments are guaranteed placement assistance for 6 months post-graduation. If not placed within this period with our support, we offer a partial fee refund as per policy terms. Our 95% placement rate demonstrates our commitment to student success.

Yes, our Executive MBA program is specifically designed for working professionals. Classes are conducted on weekends (Saturday-Sunday) with online weekday sessions. The 18-month program allows you to continue your job while earning your MBA degree. We also offer flexible attendance policies for corporate-sponsored candidates.

We offer multiple scholarship programs: Merit-based (up to 30% for 85%+ academic scores), Need-based financial aid (up to 20% for economically weaker sections), Women Excellence Scholarship (15% for all women candidates), and Early Bird discount (10% for applications before March 31st). Scholarships can be combined up to a maximum of 40% total benefit.

We accept scores from CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, and GMAT. Additionally, we conduct our own SMS Varanasi Management Aptitude Test (SMAT) for candidates who haven't appeared for national exams. The entrance exam score is one component of our holistic admission process that also includes GD, PI, and academic records.
